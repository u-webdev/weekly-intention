# Privacy Policy

Weekly Intention does not collect, store, or share any personal data.

## Data Collection
The app does not collect any personal or identifiable information.

## iCloud
Weekly Intention uses iCloud to sync your intentions across your devices.
All data is stored in your personal iCloud account and is not accessible to the developer.

## Tracking
The app does not use tracking technologies and does not track users across apps or websites.

## Third-Party Services
Weekly Intention does not use third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.
